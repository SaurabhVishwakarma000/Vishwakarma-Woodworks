How a contract runs

Four stages, start to handover.

This is the order every job actually follows — we don't skip the measuring stage, even for small work.

01

Consult

You describe the work — over a call or through the form below — and we understand the scope, budget range and timeline.

02

Measure

We visit the site, take exact measurements, and confirm ply grade, hardware and finish with you before any cutting starts.

03

Build

Fabrication happens at the workshop or on-site, depending on the job, with regular updates on progress.

04

Install & finish

Final fitting, hardware and polish, then a walkthrough with you before we call the job complete.
